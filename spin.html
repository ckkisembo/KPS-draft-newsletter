SpinOffs

There are various sub-groups that have been formed as our community members have sought to collate around their shared passions and interests, as we continue on this journey of reconnection. Take a moment and learn more about each of the groups and consider joining one, or even spearheading the formation of a new one.

"The Next Chapter"

The Book Club has been such an incentive to read more books. This is the first time for some of us to join a book club and so far, so good.

Dramas, Thrillers, Self-help and hold your breath and squeeze the edge of your seat books are endless.

The highlight is on Friday when Calvin features, a crazy boy with an adult complex, his tantrums are out of this world.

by Lilian Hamstard

Tours

This group was started to take the vibrant spirit and camaraderie of our community up a notch through refreshing, meaningful outdoor excursions exploring Uganda’s extraordinary beauty.

Wanale Ridge, Sipi Falls, the lush Mabira Forest—stand out as ideal for short excursions. Mount Elgon and Rwenzururu ranges would require preparation and teamwork.

A tradition of regular outings would keep KPS '80–86 alumni spirit alive, so let the journey begin!

by Nathan Wasolo

Ladies

by Carol Kibukamusoke

Reunited, revived… and occasionally hot-flushed! It started with Mummy Norah’s “Banange, let’s have our own group - just the ladies!” And just like that, the spin-off was born. This lively bunch of 50+ women is juggling work, relationships, grown children, and hot flashes that could start a small fire. Once in a while we get cheeky, share heartfelt rants, wellness tips and parenting hacks, and swap hilarious adulting stories.

And then there are the moments when we get raw. Suddenly, the polite pleasantries vanish and are replaced by frustration and pain... and just like that, the chat transforms into a therapy session and a safe space all at once.

The Ladies "Brunch" on May 31, 2025, hosted by our lovely headgirl, was well attended. Amid elegant sips and tiny bites, the conversation got progressively unfiltered, the laughter long and loud...

Alumni Investment Club

This group was formed to encourage classmates who shared a formative part of their lives, to build financial strength by pooling resources and investing as a united front.

The club has established interim leadership, opened a bank account and a unit trust account, and agreed on a disciplined one-year saving period before moving into more ambitious investments.

We encourage everyone to join by expressing interest on the WhatsApp group; be a part of this journey.

by Nathan Wasolo

Business Network

In this group, members have a space to exchange ideas on topical economic, entrepreneurial, and investment issues.

The impact of the group is already significant, as the consistent dialogue about wealth creation and financial growth was the breeding ground for the formation of an investment club.

The group continues to take shape as alumni bonds are strengthened through sharing insights, learning from one another’s experiences, and teasing possible collaboration opportunities.

by Nathan Wasolo
